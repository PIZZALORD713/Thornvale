# Narrative

## Premise

You arrive in Thornvale for a fresh start—or to follow something that called you here.

## Themes

* Belonging vs autonomy
* Kindness as control
* Routine as comfort vs routine as trap

## End States (High-level)

* **Assimilate:** become a pillar of the town
* **Escape:** break the loop and leave
* **Rewrite:** change the town’s rules
